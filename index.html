CREATIVITY SMP

Now featuring Aeronautics and Create: Deepsea! Dive into a fresh server packed with the Create mod and tons of add-ons. Build your empire, automate everything, and join our growing player-driven economy.

Core Gameplay Systems

Aeronautics & Sable

Engineer your very own custom vehicles! Build and pilot everything from deep-sea submarines to fully functional helicopters using Create Aeronautics.

Warfare & Big Cannons

Fortify your base! Create Big Cannons with custom shells and radars. We also feature intense military mods like Warium and Superb Warfare.

Create & Automate

Use the main Create mod to automate most in-game items and build massive train networks. Superfarms and megafarms are totally allowed here!

Explore Space, Sea, and Everything In Between

We've built a strong server that combines industry, military combat, and economy. Take it to the ocean floor with Create: Deepsea, or fly to the stars with Cosmonautics!

  • Optimized Performance: Engineered to run on lower-end hardware. Tested perfectly on a Steam Deck allocating only 4GB of RAM!
  • Chill Community: Still a new server with a growing playerbase. We have good admins who actually listen to your mod and server suggestions.
  • Massive Automation: Build without limits. Want to create a megafarm? Go for it! Our economy supports massive automated projects.

More Add-ons Include:

Create: Nuclear, Cosmonautics, and much more. Check our Discord for the full modlist!
